The home page displays every type of communication coming to the user, from email to internal messages. Every message can be set to different level of priority, allowing the user to prioritize their work load.

When the job or the conversation is done, the user can clear it. With the left and right swipe function they can reply easily or finish it directly.

The big announcement button is for creating a quick message or starting a chat. There are premade messages for meeting call outs or if somebody is calling from the general company line.

The first couple of options are for pre-populated messages.

After they close their messages, the user can get rid of them from the homepage, but there is also a history box. So, if they need to go back to check something, they can go to their history and find it.
