Promescent

www.promescent.com

The Promescent website gave us a really good creative opportunity. The product was formulated by a urologist, and is used to help men last longer in bed.

So we made the home page really interactive and seamless. The scroll bottle on the left spins, so the product stays there throughout, and the content appears next to it with every scroll. The background has a really nice gradient effect and cool particle light effect.

The website is currently being populated with new copy.
